Video Chat with Doctors

Feeling ill? Suspect you have Covid 19? Why move from your home and possibly infect many other people with the virus. With Covaid, you can consult a doctor from the comfort of your home. If a case is suspected, an arrangement is made by the doctor for an ambulance to pick you up from your home.

Realtime statistics

Know what's happening around you through an interactive covid 19 statistics report. Real time statistics in your country and from all around the world. Updated daily by our dedicated team of experts.

Report Covid 19 cases

With the push of a button, report a suspected Covid 19 case through the app. Suspect a neighbour?, Let us know in the app and help would be on its way in no time at all.
