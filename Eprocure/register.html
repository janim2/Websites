Registration Instructions

The Ghana Water Company Limited intends to create a register of plumbing contractors. The proposed register, which will also classify contractors on the basis of resourcefulness, experience etc. will serve as the basis for awarding contracts for GWCL works in future;

  • Registration is opened to all certified Ghanaian plumbing contracting firms
  • Interested parties are invited to purchase a registration form at GHc10.00
  • A fee of GHc250.00 as the cost of processing for a NEW Plumbing Contractors License and Master Plumbers License is to be paid on submission of the completed form
  • Plumbing Contractors License and Master Plumbers License are valid for one (1) year only
  • In the case of a renewal of Licenses an amount of GHc150.00 is to be paid as a fee

Documents & Information To Be Submitted

All contractors must ensure that they have the following documents ready before applying:

  1. Government Certification

    2. Without these your application will be rejected immediately

    1. Certificate of Registration of Business
    2. Ministry of Works and Housing Certificate
    3. Current Tax Clearance Certificate (GRA)
    4. SSNIT Clearance Certificate
    5. PPA Registration Certificate


  2. Staff Certification

    3. All contractors will be required to upload documentary proof of level of education for all staff

    1. Civil Engineeres
    2. Technicians
    3. Engineers Pipe Fitters
    4. Administrative Staff
    5. Any other key personnel


  3. Job Execution


  4. Equipment Ownership

    5. All contractors will be required to provide serial numbers and date of manufacture of all equipment they own including:

    1. Pipe Testing Equipment
    2. Vehicles (Pickup, etc)
    3. Crane-type Trucks
    4. Ordinary Trucks - low loader
    5. Heavy Construction Equipment (Excavators, etc)
    6. Any other relevant tools and equipment


  5. Financial Standing

    6. All contractors are required to submit documentary proof of Funding Bank Statement
