About

The Author

Many people began to call me Tracy Dampare as soon as i was named on my Outdooring day. It was fun i guess. Wait a minute, how would i know? i was only 0 years old.Anyway, i smelt my first earthly air on the 17th of September, 2000. It was so refreshing. It makes me wonder, do babies breath while in the womb? I am now proudly in University Of Ghana, Legon,one of the Universities in West Africa studying law. So far so good.

Writing of poems is not just a hobby, it is a lifestyle. A way of expressing my thoughts, feelings, ambitions, criticisms about the occurances in my neighbourhood, community,country and the world as a whole. Feel free to browse through my collection and don't forget to leave a comment.
